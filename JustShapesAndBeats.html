HOW TO PLAY
Controls
WASD — P1 Move    Space — P1 Dash
Arrow Keys — P2 Move    Enter — P2 Dash
Escape — Pause

Local 2-Player
Both players share the screen simultaneously.
P1 is blue, P2 is orange.
Each player has their own health bar and score.
The level ends when either player dies.

Goal
Survive until the music ends. Every level has a unique set of patterns.

Hazards
● Projectiles — dodge or dash through them
● Beams — avoid the laser line
● Walls — move through the gaps
● Rings — expanding pulse rings
● Chasers — homing enemies that track you
● Sprinklers — rotating spray guns
● Waves — sweeping horizontal walls

Difficulty
Easy — 70% speed, 70% spawns, 7 HP
Normal — 100% speed, 100% spawns, 5 HP
Hard — 140% speed, 150% spawns, 3 HP
Nightmare — 200% speed, 260% spawns, 2 HP
Custom — set every parameter yourself

Progression
Beat Normal → unlock Hard → beat Hard → unlock Nightmare.
Complete a level to unlock the next one in the same tier.
Beaten levels get a gold ⭐.

Account
Sign in to sync progress across devices.
Best played on the website — running the server locally is finicky,
and even if you do, your data won't sync with the website.
Play here: jsab.onrender.com
Offline: progress saves to your browser only.
